About

Find Out More About Us

Shangilia is a non profit community based organization. The CBO is rooted in Christianity with the will to help others and benefit the community as a whole.

Community Work is our main mission

Hebrews 13:16: Do not neglect to do good and to share what you have, for such sacrifices are pleasing to God.

Shangilia first started with a group of freinds that decided to collect the money they had and visit a needy family with food stuffs, household items and good vibes. Shangilia has grown in numbers visiting more families and leaving them better then they were before. It is our pleasure help people, fellowship with them and let them know that they are not alone.
